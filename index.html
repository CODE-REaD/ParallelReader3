Language Bridge Proof of Concept

Works best with Google Chrome

You may provide texts of the same article in different languages by pasting them to the panels below, or drag-and-drop local files to the panels, or click on the "load" buttons to select files from your local disc. Or click on "Load File from Online Library" to choose an existing pairing from the online library. Language Bridge will attempt to present the two texts as equal height, synchronously scrolling panels below, and to pronounce sentences when you click on them or a series of sentences when you click-and-drag over them.

PLEASE NOTE: this program is currently Proof Of Concept ONLY; it easily fails on invalid file input as no input validity checks are yet in place.
