Parallel Reader Proof of Concept

Works best with Google Chrome

Please provide two files from your computer's local filesystem. The files should contain plain text only, of the same article or story but written in two different languages (e.g., left = English and right = German). Text should be alphanumeric only, with paragraphs separated by control characters CR,LF,CR,LF (0d, 0a, 0d, 0a). Parallel Reader will attempt to present the two texts synchronously scrolling columns below, and to pronounce words when you click on them or a series of words when you click-and-drag over them.

PLEASE NOTE: this program is currently Proof Of Concept ONLY; it easily fails on invalid file input as no input validity checks are yet in place. Also, pronunciation seems to deadlock if more than one instance at a time is running on your workstation. The remedy for this is to exit the browser instance running one of the pronunciation tasks.
